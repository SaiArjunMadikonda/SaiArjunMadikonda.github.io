# **About Me**

Hi! I'm **{{ site.author.name }}**, a Robotics Engineer and recent graduate from the University of Maryland, College Park.

I specialize in robotics, artificial intelligence, and autonomous systems. My research interests include perception, planning, and control systems for mobile robots and manipulators. I have extensive experience in developing intelligent robotic systems using ROS/ROS2, implementing deep learning solutions, and creating efficient control algorithms.
